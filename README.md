# 🦆 Waddles: Your Sassy Productivity Companion

I've always struggled to keep track of my career, fitness goals, and hobbies all in one place. Existing productivity apps either completely missed the mark for my workflow, or hid the tools I actually needed behind an expensive premium paywall. Frustrated by the lack of options, I decided to take matters into my own hands and vibe-coded my ultimate dream productivity app from scratch!

Naturally, the mascot is my favorite bird: **the duck**. Why? Because just like a duck can **fly, walk, and swim**, this app is built to help you master every different aspect of your life. 

---

## ✨ Features

* 🦆 **Sassy Mascot Accountability:** Waddles isn't just a logo; he's your manager. A background worker checks your progress every 2 hours and sends custom, sassy push notifications to keep you off the couch and on your grind.
* 📅 **Daily Dashboard:** Track your daily tasks by category (Fitness, Learning, Hobbies, Misc). Watch Waddles react and your progress bar fill up as you crush your to-do list.
* 🗓️ **Weekly Planner:** Map out your growth pillars in advance. Select any day of the week and assign categorized tasks so you always know what's coming.
* 🔥 **Habit Tracker:** Build daily routines and maintain visual 🔥 streaks. Missed a day? Use the time-travel arrows to go back and log it.
* 📚 **Goal & Project Tracker:** Break massive goals into bite-sized units (e.g., a 50-video course). 
  * **Optional Deadlines:** Set a target date and get a live "days left" countdown (which turns red if you're overdue!).
  * **Daily Integration:** Send specific goal units straight to your Daily To-Do list with the click of a button.
* ⏱️ **Live Focus Timer:** Use a Stopwatch or a 25-minute Pomodoro timer. Lock the timer to a specific daily task, and when you finish, the app automatically logs your hours and checks the task off your list. (Includes a 5-minute anti-cheat system!).
* 📊 **Deep Analytics:** Visualize your time with weekly bar charts, monthly/yearly category pie charts, and a scrollable log of every focus session you've done this year.
* 🏆 **Hall of Fame:** A digital display cabinet for your 100% completed goals, categorized perfectly so you can admire your Mastery Level.
* 🔒 **100% Offline & Private:** Built on a robust, lightning-fast local Room database. Your data stays on your phone, forever. No internet required.

## How to Use Waddles

Waddles is divided into 6 main tabs at the bottom of your screen:

1. **Daily (Checkmark Icon):** This is your main dashboard. Check off your tasks here to fill up your daily progress bar. You can also quickly add "Misc" tasks for things that pop up during the day.
2. **Weekly (Calendar Icon):** Select a day (S-M-T-W-T-F-S), choose a category, and type a task to plan your week in advance. 
3. **Habits (Heart Icon):** Add daily routines (like "Drink 2L Water"). Tap the circle every day to watch your streak grow.
4. **Goals (Menu Icon):** Create big projects. If you want to work on a goal *today*, click **"Schedule for Today"** on the goal card to push it to your Daily tab. Once you complete all the units, it moves to your Trophies!
5. **Tracker (Play Icon):** Time to focus. Select what you are working on, hit **Start**, and put your phone down. When you are done, click **Stop & Save** to permanently log those hours into your stats.
6. **Trophies (Star Icon):** Sit back and admire the massive goals you have completely finished.

## How to Download & Install (APK)

Since this is an indie app, you can install Waddles directly on your Android device using the APK file provided in this repository.
Install and download the app. 
Open Waddles. **Make sure to grant the Notification Permission** when it asks, or Waddles won't be able to send you his sassy motivational reminders!
